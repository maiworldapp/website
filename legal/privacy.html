MAIWORLD Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

MAIWORLD respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is used when you use MAIWORLD services.

Information We Collect

MAIWORLD may collect information you provide, including account details, profile information, listings, messages, ratings, comments, and location information used to organise local connections.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide the service, connect users, improve safety, maintain accounts, and improve the MAIWORLD experience.

Location

Location information helps organise local feeds. Exact addresses are not displayed publicly and should only be shared by users when they choose to arrange a deal.

Payments and Transactions

MAIWORLD does not process payments, shipping, or take commissions. Users arrange their own transactions.

Data Protection

MAIWORLD takes reasonable steps to protect user information. Users should also protect their account details.

Your Choices

You may request updates or deletion of your account information through MAIWORLD contact channels.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact MAIWORLD through the official contact page.
