What is MAIWORLD?

MAIWORLD is being built to make it easier for local people to connect with one another without complicated marketplace systems or selling fees.

Buy

Discover items offered by people in your local community.

Sell

Create a simple local listing and connect directly with interested buyers.

Give Away

Offer useful items for free instead of letting them go to waste.

Find Services

Find local people offering services or let the community know what help you need.

Made to stay simple

MAIWORLD connects people. Buyers and sellers arrange their own collection, payment and private details between themselves.

No listing fees

People can create listings without paying a listing fee.

No commissions

MAIWORLD does not take a percentage from private deals.

Local first

The experience is designed around towns, cities and communities.
