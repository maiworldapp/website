# MAIWORLD Build In Public Guide

## Our Mission

We are documenting the real journey of building MAIWORLD from the ground up.

The goal is not to appear perfect.

The goal is to show genuine progress, celebrate milestones, learn from mistakes, and build trust with the community.

---

## Content Principles

- Be honest
- Show real progress
- Explain what was learned
- Celebrate small wins
- Share challenges
- Never fake success
- Never exaggerate

---

## Every Post Should Answer

- What did we build?
- Why does it matter?
- What did we learn?
- What comes next?

---

## Media Checklist

Before capturing screenshots or videos:

- Clean workspace
- Hide personal information
- Hide email addresses
- Hide passwords
- Hide API keys
- Close unrelated tabs
- Ensure MAIWORLD branding is visible

---

## Quality Standard

Every piece of content must pass the MAIWORLD Quality Review before publishing.

Minimum score:

**9.5/10**
